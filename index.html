Time and Place

The beer club is located at the 3rd floor of Navet and takes place at 16:00-later on the last Friday every month. There might also be beer clubs anytime else during the month if someone arranges it, and it that case it's noted in the calendar below.

Sign up for our mailing list to make sure you never miss a beerclub! Don't worry nothing but montly reminders and critical information from the board will be posted to the list! If you cange your mind, unsubscribe here.
