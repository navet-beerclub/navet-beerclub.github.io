Time and Place

The beer club is located at the 1st or 3rd floor of Navet and takes place at 16:00-later. We aim to have a beer club on the last Friday every month, but if we are too busy it might not happen. There might also be beer clubs anytime else during the month if someone arranges it, and it that case it's noted in the calendar below.

Mailing List

Subscribe to our mailing list to make sure you never miss a beerclub! Don't worry nothing but montly reminders and critical information from the board will be posted to the list! If you change your mind, unsubscribe here.

Manual instructions: Send an email from the email address you want to subscrive with to sympa@lists.uu.se with the subject subscribe scilifelab-beerclub to subscribe, or unsubscribe scilifelab-beerclub to unsubscribe.

Contact Us

Do you have any suggestions for the beer club, or beer recommendations? Or maybe you want to help out organizing the beer clubs? Feel free to send us an email or come talk to us in the lunch room.
